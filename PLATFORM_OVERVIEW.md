# COMPASSID Platform Overview

## Mission Statement

COMPASSID is a comprehensive digital platform designed to accelerate conservation science by connecting researchers, centralizing critical research data, and providing intelligent tools to discover insights, secure funding, and collaborate on solutions to the world's most pressing environmental challenges.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

### Pain Points in Conservation Science

**1. Fragmented Research Landscape**
- Conservation research is scattered across multiple databases (PubMed, CrossRef, OpenAlex, etc.)
- No single source of truth for finding relevant conservation papers
- Difficult to discover research across species, ecosystems, methods, and geographic regions
- Researchers spend countless hours manually searching through disconnected systems

**2. Metadata Quality Crisis**
- Research papers often lack structured conservation-specific metadata
- Critical details (species studied, ecosystems, threats, conservation actions) are buried in full text
- Impossible to filter or analyze papers by conservation-relevant dimensions
- Metadata extraction requires domain expertise and is time-consuming

**3. Knowledge Discovery Gap**
- Hard to identify research gaps and emerging trends
- No way to synthesize findings across multiple studies
- Collaboration opportunities are hidden
- Limited visibility into what's being studied where and by whom

**4. Funding Accessibility**
- Grant opportunities are difficult to discover
- No centralized database of conservation funding
- Researchers struggle to find relevant funding sources
- Grant writing is time-consuming and requires specialized skills

**5. Career & Networking Challenges**
- Conservation job opportunities are fragmented
- Difficult to find collaborators with specific expertise
- Limited professional networking infrastructure
- Hard to showcase research impact and build reputation

---

## What COMPASSID Does

COMPASSID is the **all-in-one platform for conservation science** that brings together research discovery, intelligent analytics, funding opportunities, career development, and professional networking into a unified, AI-powered ecosystem.

## Core Features

### 1. Intelligent Research Database (500K+ Papers)

**Comprehensive Conservation Research Archive**
- Aggregates research from PubMed, CrossRef, and OpenAlex
- Continuously updated with weekly imports of new publications
- Rich metadata extraction including:
  - Species and taxonomic groups studied
  - Geographic locations (countries, regions, protected areas)
  - Ecosystems (Marine, Forests, Grasslands, Wetlands, etc.)
  - Research methods (Camera Traps, Remote Sensing, eDNA, etc.)
  - Threats (Habitat Loss, Climate Change, Poaching, etc.)
  - Conservation actions (Protected Areas, Restoration, Community-Based Conservation, etc.)
  - Policy frameworks (SDGs, CBD, Paris Agreement, CITES, etc.)
  - Study types (Field Study, Meta-Analysis, Modeling, etc.)

**AI-Powered Natural Language Search** (ðŸš€ Flagship Feature)
- Search using plain English: "show me camera trap studies about tigers in India from the last 5 years"
- AI understands intent and extracts structured filters automatically
- Handles geographic name variations (e.g., "Saudi Arabia" matches "Kingdom of Saudi Arabia")
- Smart filter prioritization prevents zero-result searches
- Sort and limit controls: "find me Troy Sternberg's highest cited paper, just one"
- Powered by Claude 3.5 Haiku (cost: ~$0.001 per query)

**Advanced Filtering & Search**
- Filter by species, locations, ecosystems, methods, threats, conservation actions
- Date range filtering (by year or specific month)
- Author search with exclusion support
- Citation-based sorting (highest/lowest cited)
- Date-based sorting (newest/oldest first)
- Interactive map visualization of research locations

**Community-Enhanced Metadata**
- Researchers can submit metadata suggestions for papers
- Peer review system for metadata improvements
- Crowdsourced data quality ensures accuracy
- Track your contributions and impact

## Technology Stack

### Backend
- **Node.js + Express** - RESTful API
- **PostgreSQL** - Primary database with JSONB for flexible metadata
- **Claude 3.5 Haiku (Anthropic)** - AI-powered natural language search and insights
- **PubMed, CrossRef, OpenAlex APIs** - Research data aggregation
- **Stripe** - Payment processing for subscriptions

### Infrastructure
- Automated weekly paper imports
- Bulk import scripts for historical data
- Metadata extraction pipelines
- SEO optimization and sitemap generation
